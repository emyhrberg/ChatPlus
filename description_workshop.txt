[h1] Chat+ [/h1]
Chat+ makes Terraria's chat smoother, smarter and more fun with emojis, images, links, text editing shortcuts and more!

[h1] Features [/h1] 
[list]
[*] [b]Emoji Support[/b]: Access thousands of emojis directly in chat, powered by [url=https://emojibase.dev/emojis?] Emojibase [/url].
[*] [b]Image Support[/b]: Upload and send your own images!
[*] [b]Link Support[/b]: Links you type in chat become clickable.
[*] [b]Autocomplete Commands[/b]: Type [code] "/" [/code] into chat to autocomplete commands. Press Tab to insert the selected command.
[*] [b]Autocomplete Tags[/b]: Type any tag to open autocomplete window. See the [url=https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chat#Tags] wiki [/url] for all tags supported.
[*] [b]Text Editing Shortcuts[/b]: Use common text-editing shortcuts like Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, and more directly in chat.

[/list]

[h2] New Tag: Emojis [/h2] 
Enter [code] : [/code] into chat to insert emojis. Press Tab to insert the selected emoji.

[h2] New Tag: Upload Images [/h2] 
Enter [code] @ [/code] into chat to upload your images. Press Tab to insert the selected image.
